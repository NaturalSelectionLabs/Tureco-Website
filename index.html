About Us

AI for Web3

Tureco is an R&D group committed to building a public and decentralized recommender system based on privacy-preserving machine learning techniques for Web3.

Our Goal

We aim to tackle web3-specific challenges in sifting through content on decentralized platforms and helping Web3 users locate what matters most to them, e.g., fashionable topics, public figures, or trading behavior. We are committed to returning AI models to the user and training them based on the user's will.

System Design

Our system is designed in Web3 style, decentralized, user-owned, and privacy-preserving. Unlike the general business recommendation system, only the publicly available data will be used to take care of the privacy concern. It is a fully decentralized recommend system, which is flexible, efficient, and extensible. We promise that all projects will be publicly available to everyone.
