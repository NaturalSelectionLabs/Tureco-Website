About Us

AI for Web3

Tureco is an R&D group committed to building a public and decentralized recommendation system based on privacy-preserving machine learning techniques for Web3.

Our Goal

We aim to tackle web3-specific challenges in sifting through content on decentralized platforms and helping Web3 users locate what matters the most to them, e.g., fashionable topics, public figures, or trading behaviors. The trained AI models will be retained locally, the user has the right to opt out at any time.

System Design

Our system is designed with web3 in mind: decentralized, user-owned, and privacy-preserving. Unlike typical recommendation systems used by web3 companies, only the publicly available data will be used to take care of the privacy concern. It is a fully decentralized recommendation system, which is flexible, efficient, and extensible. All our all projects will be made open source.

Techniques

We aim to apply the state-of-the-art intelligence system based on the novel collaborative learning techniques to enhance users' experience with no privacy anxiety while exploring Web3. We will adopt advanced technologies such as Differential Privacy, Homomorphic Encryption and Collaborative Learning to ensure a safe and comfortable Web3 recommendation experience.

Trending

Our system provides a trending function by checking out the trending topics of all recent contents in Twitter, JiKe and RSS3 networks, including Mirror, Ethereum NFT, Polygon NFT, Gitcoin Donation, xDai POAP, etc.
